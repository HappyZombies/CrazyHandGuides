About Us

Hello everyone, I am so glad you stopped by this website.

This site is intended to hold a sort of 'database' of the 'best' videos out there for each character. Each video is 'hand picked' and considered to be a good video that displays a characters skill. If you see a video that you feel like shouldn't be here, be sure to report it!

The site is about 90% done, just need to fix some small issues and it should be completed!

I still want more videos! If you see a video of a match or a montage that you feel like should be here, please send it to us!
